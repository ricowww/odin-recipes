Description

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat. It is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups old rice
  • 4 eggs beaten
  • garlic
  • onions
  • 1 shrimp bouillon cube in 1/4 cup water

    • Steps

    1. Cook eggs in hot wok until flaky
    2. Remove eggs, heat oil til smoky
    3. Add rice, press firmly to wok
    4. Add garlic and onions, toss
    5. Add shrimp stock and eggs, toss
